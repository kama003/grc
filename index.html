Solang Valley, Himachal Pardesh

Guha Research Conference

Nestled in the upper reaches of Kullu district in Himachal Pradesh, Solang Valley is located 14 kilometers from Manali (altitude 2,560 meter/ 8,400 feet). The majestic Valley is renowned for its views of glaciers, snow caped mountains and undulating meadows. It is a hub for adventure activities including river rafting, paragliding, snow trekking, snowboarding, hiking, trekking and camping.
